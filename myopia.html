Normal Vision vs. Myopic Vision

Experience the difference between normal vision and myopic vision. Toggle between the two views to understand how myopia affects distant objects.

Normal Vision

How Vision Differs

Normal Eye: Focuses light rays directly on the retina, resulting in a clear image at all distances.

Myopic Eye: The eyeball is elongated or the cornea is too curved, causing light to focus in front of the retina. This leads to blurry distant vision.

Correction: Concave lenses spread the light rays outward so they can focus properly on the retina.
